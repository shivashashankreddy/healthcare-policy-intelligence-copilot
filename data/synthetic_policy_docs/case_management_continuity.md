# Synthetic Case Management Continuity Policy

This synthetic operations policy supports caseworker workflows for claims and authorization follow-up.

Caseworkers should provide plain-language summaries of policy requirements, identify the next required documentation step, and route complex clinical or compliance issues to the appropriate reviewer.

Case notes should not include real patient names, member identifiers, social security numbers, payment account numbers, or confidential payer data. Notes may reference synthetic case identifiers during testing.

When a policy answer includes exceptions or case-by-case language, caseworkers should avoid promising approval and should document that final decisions require review against the applicable synthetic policy criteria.
