# Synthetic Claims Timely Filing and Reconsideration Policy

This synthetic claims policy defines sample timely filing and reconsideration handling rules for demonstration only.

Standard professional and facility claims must be received within 120 calendar days from the date of service. Corrected claims must be received within 60 calendar days from the original adjudication date.

A claim may be denied for timely filing when:

- The received date is later than the applicable filing window.
- No acceptable proof of timely submission is attached.
- The claim is not part of an approved exception category.

Acceptable proof may include a synthetic clearinghouse acceptance report, prior rejection evidence showing corrected routing, or documented outage notice. Internal notes alone are not sufficient proof.

Claims involving coordination of benefits may receive an additional 90 calendar days after the primary payer explanation date when the explanation date is included in the claim record.
