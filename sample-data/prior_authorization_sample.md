# Synthetic Prior Authorization Policy for Advanced Imaging

This synthetic policy applies to requests for MRI, CT, PET, and other advanced imaging services. It is not based on any real payer policy and must not be used for real patient care.

Authorization may be approved when the request includes:

- Ordering clinician specialty and reason for study.
- Relevant symptoms, duration, and prior conservative treatment.
- Results of prior X-ray, ultrasound, lab testing, or specialist evaluation when applicable.
- Clear clinical question that the imaging study is expected to answer.
- Confirmation that duplicate imaging has not already addressed the same clinical question.

Requests should be pended for additional documentation when the clinical indication is missing, when conservative therapy is required but not documented, or when the requested modality does not match the stated clinical question.

Exceptions may be considered for urgent neurological symptoms, suspected malignancy, or post-operative complications. These exceptions require explicit clinician attestation.
